About me:

I love games, whether that's a board game, a video game, an outdoor game, TTRPG, escape room, you name it! Games are my passion, and I think people should know more about them. I've always felt like people don't take games seriously, and think they are predominantly for children, when in fact they can be so deep with meaning, educational, and fun all at the same time! Because of this, I've made it my goal to educate more people about games for all the benefits they can provide (including having fun)!

I'm a big fan of rules and how they interact; it's probably why I love games, math(s), programming, and so much more. Studying how each rule affects the other rules in order to reach a certain objective is deeply interesting to me, that's how you figure out theorems in maths, code something in a specific coding language, and come up with a winning strategy in games! I think there's a life where I would've been a lawyer instead.

Music is my other passion. I started playing the piano back when I was six, and still regularly do! It's so fun to play songs you've listened to countless times and jam to them. I have a playlist of my favourites with around 3000 songs which is probably around 98% video game music (I'm quite proud of that one). I listen to music when I work, travel, or anything else really. Here are some of my recent favourite ones which I'll try and update every so often! (It's already hard enough choosing a couple of favourites from recently, so there's no way I can choose a single favourite from all time!)
